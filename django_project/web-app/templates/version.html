MyCivitas Golden Albatross 1.0.0

August 2023

Introducing exciting new developements to the MyCivitas platform! We're absolutely delighted to share the exciting news that Mycivtas has now been updated to Golden Albatros 1.0.0. This transformative release represents a significant milestone, marking a substantial leap forward in our commitment to enhancing your Mycivtas experience.


Explore our enhanced tools, engage with newfound functionalities and witness a more efficient, productive, and enjoyable Mycivtas experience. Thanks to continued community support and suggestions we are able to continue developing and enhansing the myCivitas platform!.

Enhancements:

  • Optimization: Through diligent efforts, we've meticulously optimized our platform to significantly reduce the loading time for both features and maps. Our commitment to enhancing user experience has driven us to streamline the performance, ensuring that you can now access and interact with your favorite features and maps swiftly and seamlessly.
